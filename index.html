Packing & prep checklist

  • Both kids' uniforms (home + away jerseys, shorts, socks)
  • Cleats + indoor/turf shoes (Dome Field 02A on Sunday may want turf-appropriate footwear)
  • Shin guards, mouthguards, headbands
  • Multiple water bottles per kid + electrolyte mix
  • Snacks: fruit, bagels, granola bars, pretzels
  • Sunscreen + hats (Saturday outdoor games)
  • Folding chairs, blanket, small cooler
  • Rain gear / extra layers (May weather in PA is unpredictable)
  • First aid: KT tape, band-aids, ibuprofen, ice packs
  • Phone chargers / portable battery
  • Cash for parking / concessions
  • Schedule printout + this plan
